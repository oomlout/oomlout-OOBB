OOBB - OOpen Building Block System.

The OOpen Building Block System. A set of laser cutable components that can be bolted together to make any number of things. Relying on a 20 mm hole spacing and standard M6 hardware.

Notes *Main Bolt Size M6 x 12 *Laser Cutter Style: **Black --> Cut Line **Orange --> Etch Line Types *BP - Bearing Plate *CI - Circles *GE - Gears *HL - Fully enclosed holders. *JA - Jacks *SP - Spacer Plate *MM - Motor Mounts *MP - Mounting Plates *PL - Plates *PT - Plate *SC - Shaft Couplers
